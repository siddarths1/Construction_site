BUILDERS started its journey in the precast industry in the year 2018 and is committed to take strides to become the leader in the arena of precast concrete construction by embarking on modern and sophisticated technology. Its our mission to continually strive to provide coast - effective and high quality products that exceed customer expectation and help us achieve our business vision. We have a very strong foothold and customer base all around India and are one of the few companies to offer end to end solution (Design to Delivery) in the precast Industry. Currently, our state of art manufacturing facilities are located at four locations pan India with Headquarters at Salem, Tamilnadu. With varied business interests, we have come a long way. Today, we have established our expertise in Real Estate, Villas, Individual houses, Apartments. Backed by the efforts of our 100 employees, the patronage of our esteemed customers and support of our reliable vendors, we hope to make greater strides in whatever venture we undertake, in the years to come.
